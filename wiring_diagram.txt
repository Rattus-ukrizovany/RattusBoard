Column assignments:                        Row assignments (independent per half):

TRRS Wiring:
Tip (T)    - Serial communication (GP1)
Ring 1 (R) - Ground (GND)  
Ring 2 (R) - Power (3.3V)
Sleeve (S) - Not connected

Diode Direction: COL2ROW (cathode toward row, anode toward column)
